In each round, the payoffs are as follows. The Receiver knows these payoffs too.

Your payoff: Your payoff corresponds exactly to the guess of the Receiver in this round.

Your payoff = 4 - (guess of the Receiver)

Simply put, you earn more when the Receiver guesses a lower secret number.

Receiver's payoff: The Receiver's payoff depends on how close their guess is to the secret number.

Receiver's payoff = 3 - |guess - secret number|

where |guess - secret number| is the distance between the guess and the secret number in the round.

Simply put, the Receiver earns more when their guess is closer to the true secret number.

Part 2 ends after 10 rounds. One of the 10 rounds will be randomly selected at the end of the experiment for effective payment in this part.

Press Next to begin the game.
