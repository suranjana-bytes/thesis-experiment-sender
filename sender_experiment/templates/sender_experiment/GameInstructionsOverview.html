Description of the game: each round of the game has 4 steps.

Step 1: The computer program generates a secret number, which is 1, 2 or 3.

Step 2: You are informed of the secret number. The Receiver does not know it.

Step 3: You can send information about the secret number to the Receiver.

Step 4: The Receiver sees your information and then makes a guess.
