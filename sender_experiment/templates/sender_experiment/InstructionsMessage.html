Before the Receiver guesses the secret number, you can send them information about it.

This information will take the form of a set of numbers, with the only constraint that the secret number must be part of the set.

For example, if the secret number is 3, you may send one of the following sets:

  • {1, 2, 3}
  • {2, 3}
  • {3}

Press Next to continue.
